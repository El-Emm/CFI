Salvation

Accept Jesus Today

Are you ready for Jesus to change your life? You are one prayer away from forgiveness, peace, and everlasting hope.

The Gospel in Simple Truth

God loves you. Jesus Christ died for your sins, rose again, and offers you new life. You do not have to carry guilt, fear, or emptiness any longer. Turn to Him, believe in your heart, and confess Jesus as Lord.

“If you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.” Romans 10:9

Pray This Prayer

Lord Jesus, I admit I am a sinner. I believe You died for me and rose again. I turn from my old ways and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Fill me with Your Holy Spirit. Help me follow You all the days of my life. Amen.
