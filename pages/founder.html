Founder & Visionary

Meet Evangelist Ebel Philips

Faith leader, humanitarian, and global advocate for the vulnerable.

Vision & Calling

Evangelist Ebel Philips answered a divine calling to serve those the world often overlooks — widows without support, children without school fees, families without food, and communities without hope. What began as grassroots compassion has grown into CharityFaith International, a US-registered nonprofit reaching nations across Africa and beyond.

"Faith without action is incomplete. We are called to love in word and in deed — meeting both spiritual and physical needs."

Humanitarian Mission

Under her leadership, CFI delivers healthcare assistance, education support, food distribution, widow empowerment, shelter projects, small business startup support, and faith-based outreach. Each program reflects a commitment to dignity, sustainability, and Christ-centered compassion.

Faith Leadership & Global Outreach

Evangelist Ebel has led numerous crusades and community gatherings where the gospel is shared alongside practical aid. Her ministry bridges continents — mobilizing churches, partners, and donors to fuel transformation in vulnerable communities worldwide.

Dr. Uchenna Collins Ojukwu

Pastor, Charity Faith International

Dr. Uchenna Collins Ojukwu is a Pastor, medical professional, public health advocate, and humanitarian leader committed to advancing both spiritual and physical well-being. He serves with Charity Faith International, where he promotes evangelism, discipleship, medical missions, and community outreach.

As a researcher, author, and scientific reviewer, he has contributed to numerous peer-reviewed publications in public health and infectious diseases. He has led medical missions and humanitarian programs among underserved communities across different nations. His mission is to bring hope, healing, and the love of Christ to people through faith, service, and compassionate action.
